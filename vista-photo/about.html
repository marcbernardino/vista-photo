About me

My name is Caleb Hall and I have been capturing lifelong memories for my clients for over 20 years. I’m currently based out of Phoenix, Arizona and I got my bachelor’s degree in photography and videography at the New York film academy. I travel through-out Arizona for my clients including but not limited to: Mesa, Chandler, Phoenix, Gilbert, and Scottsdale. I have a dog named Hercules and a cat named Lester. Please contact me if you have any questions!
